What is Career Counseling?

Career counseling is a process that assists you in understanding yourself and the world around you in order to make career decisions as well as educational, personal, and professional decisions. Developing a career entails more than just deciding on a major and what job you want to get after graduation. To find the best career match, we combine personality and interests with abilities. It is not required that what you are capable of doing will make you happy. The goal of career counseling is to help you make the right decision by providing you with enough information to make educated decisions.

Why you should get your career counseling done?

As a society, we now place a higher value on mental health than ever before. Today, all we want is happiness. The truth is that our professional lives have a significant impact on our mental health! It's a nightmare to be stuck in a job that isn't right for you.
